♻️ Plastic Waste Recycling & Reuse Bin

Objective

Convert plastic waste into reusable eco-bricks.

Materials

  • Plastic Drum / Box
  • Compression Lever
  • Brick Mold
  • Label Stickers

Working Principle

  1. Insert plastic waste
  2. Compress using lever
  3. Form eco-brick
  4. Remove mold
  5. Eco-brick ready

Applications

  • Benches
  • Pavements
  • Small structures

Advantages

  • Reduces plastic pollution
  • Low-cost solution
  • Eco-friendly & reusable

Conclusion

Turning plastic waste into useful eco-bricks!
